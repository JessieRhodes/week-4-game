Number to Guess:

A random number will appear at the beginning of each game. By clicking on a crystal, a specific amount will be added to your score.


The value of each crystal is hidden until you click it!


To win the game, your total score must match the random number. If your score goes above the random number, you lose the game. When the game restarts, the random number and the value of each crystal will change.
